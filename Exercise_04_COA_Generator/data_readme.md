### **1. THE TARGET: VILLAGE PAMPUR**
*   **Intelligence:** A group of 4x Foreign Terrorists (FTs) belonging to 'Lashkar-e-Taiba' have taken shelter in a wood-and-mud dhok (seasonal shepherd hut) located inside a dense apple orchard at Grid 56N KM 1144 2233.
*   **Weaponry:** Targets are armed with 2x Pika guns (Machine guns), AK-47s, and have reportedly wired the entrances with basic IEDs (Improvised Explosive Devices).
*   **HVT (High Value Target):** Group includes 'Tiger-01', a known IED expert responsible for several MSR attacks.

### **2. TERRAIN & ENVIRONMENT**
*   **The Orchard:** Approx 2 acres of dense apple trees. Low visibility (max 15 meters) at eye level due to foliage. The dhok is located in the exact center, slightly elevated.
*   **Surroundings:**
    *   **North:** Open paddy fields for 200 meters, then a fast-flowing canal.
    *   **East/West:** Residential houses (Pukka structures) start within 50 meters of the orchard fence.
    *   **South:** The village graveyard and a thicket of poplar trees.
*   **Civilian Presence:** The residential houses on the East and West are occupied by approx 40-50 civilians. Three of these houses are within the direct line of fire if PT (Precision Target) fire is used.
*   **Weather:** Overcast. First light at 0530h. Current time: 0200h.

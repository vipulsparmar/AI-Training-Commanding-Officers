### **1. CHRONOLOGICAL LOG OF EVENTS (0400h - 1400h)**

#### **0400h - 0600h: Cordon and Initial Contact**
*   **0400h:** Based on specific HUMINT (Reliability B-1) regarding the presence of 2x Hardcore Terrorists (HCT) in village Hajipura, 15 RR launched a Cordon and Search Operation (CASO).
*   **0430h:** Inner cordon established by Alpha and Bravo Coys. Outer cordon by local Police and CRPF (Central Reserve Police Force).
*   **0515h:** First contact established. Fire opened from a double-story brick house (Grid UR 5560 2230) on the leading section of the search party.
*   **0545h:** One NCO (Havildar Kumar) sustained a gunshot wound (GSW) to the left shoulder. First aid administered. Non-life threatening but requires evacuation.

#### **0600h - 0900h: Escalation and Civil Unrest**
*   **0615h:** Terrorists identified as 'Abu Hamza' (Foreign Terrorist) and 'Sajid' (Local). Both high-value targets on the "Most Wanted" list.
*   **0645h:** Crowds (approx 150-200 civilians) started gathering at the village periphery near the CRPF outer cordon. Chanting slogans. Stones thrown.
*   **0730h:** Internet suspended in the district as a precautionary measure to prevent 'overground workers' (OGWs) from mobilizing more crowds.
*   **0815h:** Intermittent firing continues. Terrorists using automatic weapons (AK-47) and possibly a UBGL (Under Barrel Grenade Launcher).
*   **0845h:** 15 RR Ghatak Platoon positioned for surgical breach if required.

#### **0900h - 1200h: Logistics and Casualty Evacuation**
*   **0915h:** CASEVAC requested. However, the arterial road (NH-44) is blocked by a civilian protest (approx 500 people) 10km from the battalion location. 
*   **0945h:** Regimental Medical Officer (RMO) reports Havildar Kumar's condition is stable but blood loss is increasing. Requesting aerial CASEVAC (Army Aviation - Cheetah) if possible.
*   **1030h:** Ammo resupply convoy (First-Line Ammo) delayed due to narrow village lanes and civilian interference. Currently 2km out.
*   **1115h:** Encounter house on fire (partially). Smoke making thermal visibility difficult.

#### **1200h - 1400h: Intelligence and Command Updates**
*   **1215h:** Intercept suggests a third terrorist might be hiding in the cowshed adjacent to the main house. Code heard: *"Zaroori nikaal"* (Emergency exit).
*   **1245h:** Police report that a mob is moving towards the BHQ to protest the CASO. Reinforcements requested for the Base security.
*   **1315h:** One civilian injured by "stray bullet" near the outer cordon. Medics treating. Situation tense.
*   **1345h:** CO 15 RR (Colonel Sharma) on site. Directing the Ghatak Platoon to maintain restraint but prepare for final intervention before sunset.
